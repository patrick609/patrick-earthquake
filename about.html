About Earthquakes

Understanding the science behind earthquakes and their effects.

What Causes Earthquakes?

Earthquakes are caused by the sudden release of energy in the Earth's crust, which creates seismic waves. This release of energy can happen due to natural causes such as plate tectonics or volcanic activity.

Earthquake Safety

During an earthquake, it's essential to stay calm, take cover, and protect yourself from falling objects. Stay away from windows and doors, and if outside, move to an open area.
